## Handwriting recognition

- [HanziLookupJS](https://github.com/gugray/HanziLookupJS) powers the Draw-mode
  character recognition (`public/hanzilookup.min.js`, `public/mmah.json`).

## Pronunciation audio

- Audio files under `public/audio/` are synthesized with Microsoft Edge's
  online neural text-to-speech via [`msedge-tts`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/msedge-tts).
